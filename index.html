Features of Video on Demand Platform

Easy Access

A student will get a link to download VOD software—an online portal—on registered email address within 24 to 48 hours of purchase of course from official website. Register and Sign-up on the portal to access courses.

Simple Layout

One of the distinguishing feature of our software is, it has a very simple layout which allows a user to access subjects, discussions, doubts, and downloads modules very easily. In addition, the software is very easy to navigate.

Bookmarks

Bookmark feature in our VOD software allows you to bookmark videos as per the progress like you can mark the video which you want to watch later and also mark videos as completed if you don’t want to watch them later.

Video Player

Our video player is engineered specifically for VOD services to provide you with a flawless playback experience. The player has features including rewind 10s, volume control, speed control, video quality control, pause and play etc.

Ask Your Query

This is a very important feature which a student can use to post any subject related queries to subject experts while watching the video. Besides, the time snapping feature tracks the time on which you had a query.

Lecture PDF

This unique feature allows a user to access workbook questions which are being explained in the respective video. The questions open in a multi-featured PDF reader in which you can even highlight and mark any doubts.
